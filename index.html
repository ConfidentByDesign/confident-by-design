How it works

Three steps to your next gig

Build your confidence profile
Select your skills and rate how confident you feel in each one — from 1 (still learning) to 5 (this is your zone). Takes about 2 minutes.
2
See your ranked matches
Opportunities are scored and ranked based on how well they fit your skills and your readiness level. Strong matches float to the top. Growth opportunities are clearly labelled.

Use the AI toolkit to land it
Every listing comes with four AI-powered tools: a fit analysis, a word-for-word negotiation script, a pitch email, and a client vetting checklist. Everything you need to go from interested to hired.

Our Story

Built for women
who mean business.

Confident by Design exists because talented women deserve more than a job board. They deserve a platform that sees their full potential.

✦

The Mission

To close the confidence gap between talented women and the opportunities they deserve — one match, one negotiation, one gig at a time.

◎

The Problem

Most job platforms match on skills alone. But skills without confidence don't get you hired. We built something different — a matcher that honours both.

About the Founder

Hi, I'm Kim.

I've spent years watching brilliant women undersell themselves, take gigs that don't fit, and leave money on the table because nobody taught them they didn't have to. Confident by Design is my answer to that.

This platform is built on a simple belief: the right opportunity matched to a woman who knows her worth — and has the tools to act on it — changes everything. Not just for her, but for every client she serves and every door she opens.
